About Us

Our Story

Peter’s Tax & Bookkeeping Solution was founded by Peter Navin Prasad, a dedicated tax professional serving clients since 2016.

Based in Fairfield, California, we serve individuals, business owners, and real estate investors throughout the Bay Area, with a strong and established presence in Solano County.

Since 2015, we have assisted more than 400 clients with tax preparation, bookkeeping, and financial support services.

We believe trust is earned through clear communication, honest guidance, and dependable results.

Our approach is thorough, detail-driven, and focused on long-term client relationships.
